This Privacy Policy explains how TubeAlert collects, uses, and protects information while providing YouTube notification services on Discord.

Only the data necessary for the operation of TubeAlert is stored. Information may be removed when it is no longer required or upon request where applicable.

Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect stored information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
